Special Initiatives

Overflow Offline

Stack Overflow, in partnership with a non-profit organization
named Kiwix, worked to update and upgrade access to an offline
version of our question and answer database.

For developers and technologists around the world, Stack Overflow
represents the greatest living encyclopedia of knowledge available
on the art and practice of programming, with over 50 million
questions asked and answered.

With the release of Overflow Offline, Kiwix is able to provide a more
current and readable version of our dataset to
users who want to access this information without an internet connection.
